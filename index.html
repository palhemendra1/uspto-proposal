Cryptographic Audit Layer for Patent Lifecycle Traceability

A PCT-aligned, non-intrusive infrastructure layer for enhancing transparency and decision intelligence across the international patent lifecycle.

Review Proposal Technical Specs

1. Executive Summary

AINexForge proposes a Cryptographic Audit Layer (CAL) designed to anchor pre-filing and post-filing events using cryptographic hashing. This system improves auditability of submission timelines and document evolution without replacing official USPTO recordation systems. Our goal is to introduce an interoperable infrastructure layer that enhances applicant-side workflow efficiency and compliance readiness.

2. The Problem

Fragmeneted pre-filing documentation trails and limited verifiability of document evolution lead to administrative inefficiencies. There is a critical lack of standardized audit layers across applicant-side systems in the multi-jurisdictional PCT framework.

3. The Solution

Our CAL introduces: Pre-Filing Traceability (timestamped disclosures), Filing Event Anchoring (hash-based verification), and Post-Filing Monitoring (lifecycle tracking and intelligent alerts).
